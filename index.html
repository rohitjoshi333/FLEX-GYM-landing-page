About Us

Welcome to FLEX Nepal

Established in 2018, FLEX Nepal has become Kathmandu's premier fitness destination. Located in the heart of the city, we combine international fitness standards with local understanding to create a unique wellness experience for the Nepali community.

Our mission is to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone in Nepal, regardless of their fitness level or background. We believe in building a stronger, healthier Nepal one member at a time.
